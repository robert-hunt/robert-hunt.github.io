Teaching & Outreach

While I am not currently teaching formal courses, I am actively involved in various educational outreach activities and creating educational content. Here are some highlights:

2024 Gallery of Fluid Motion

Presentation of cutting-edge fluid dynamics research through visually stunning video submissions at the annual APS Division of Fluid Dynamics meeting.

2024 Girls Get Math at ICERM

Participation in a summer program designed to inspire high school girls to explore mathematics and its applications, hosted by the Institute for Computational and Experimental Research in Mathematics.

2023 Gallery of Soft Matter

Showcase of innovative research in soft matter physics through visually compelling video submissions at the annual APS March Meeting.

2022 Gallery of Fluid Motion

Showcase of fluid dynamics phenomena through visually captivating video entries at the APS Division of Fluid Dynamics annual meeting.

2019 Gallery of Fluid Motion

Presentation of innovative fluid dynamics research through visually compelling video submissions at the APS Division of Fluid Dynamics conference.
