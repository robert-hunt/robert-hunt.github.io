Welcome

Hello, I'm Robert Hunt, a postdoctoral researcher at Brown University specializing in fluid mechanics. My work investigates how multiphase fluids, interfaces, solids, and solutes interact in natural systems. I use a combination of custom experiments, theory, and computation to study phenomena which arise from these interactions.

Please explore the site to learn more about my work.
