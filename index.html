Welcome to the Hunt Lab at the University of Chicago! Our work investigates how multiphase fluids, interfaces, solids, and solutes interact, often motivated by ocean and atmospheric systems. We use a combination of custom experiments, modelling, and computation to study phenomena which arise from these interactions.

Please reach out if you are interested in working with us!
